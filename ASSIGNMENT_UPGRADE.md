# 🏥 Manual Assignment Upgrade
> "Power to the Admin"

## 1. Problem
- Admin couldn't select a specific doctor manually.
- Auto-assign sometimes failed silently if AI logic was too strict or dept was missing.

## 2. Solution: Interactive Selection
- **Clickable Cards:** Doctor cards in the dialog are now interactive.
- **Selection State:** Clicking a doctor highlights the card in **Blue**.
- **Dynamic Buttons:**
  - **No Selection:** Shows "✨ Auto-Assign Best Available" (AI Mode).
  - **Doctor Selected:** Shows "✓ Confirm Assignment" (Manual Mode).

## 3. How to Use
1. Click **"Assign Patient"**.
2. **Option A (AI):** Click "Auto-Assign" button immediately.
3. **Option B (Manual):**
   - Click on "Dr. Sarah Johnson".
   - Click "Confirm Assignment".
   - Patient is assigned specifically to her and moves to "In Treatment".
